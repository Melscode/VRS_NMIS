National Meat Inspection Service
Policy on Requesting / Use of Official Vehicle

1. Priority use of the vehicle shall be for NMIS Official purposes.

2. Only NMIS Official drivers and officers/employees with approved authority to drive are allowed to drive Official vehicles.

3. Office hours are from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. As a matter of policy, no transaction with the concerned staff are allowed before or after office hours unless with prior arrangement.

4. Request shall be done by properly accomplishing Request Form completely.

5. Reservation shall be on a FIRST-COME-FIRST-SERVED basis. Request for vehicle must be made at least day within Metro Manila and five(5) days if outside Metro Manila with an approved Memorandum Order/Travel Order before the trip for proper scheduling.

6. USe of vehicle for personal purpose shall require approval of the Executive Director.

7. The following is prohibited inside the vehicle:
a. Smoking
b. Eating
c. Drinking liquor or any alcoholic beverages

8. Central and Regional Drivers shall be in charge in the:
a. Proper maintenace of vehicle
b. Interior.Exterior must be maintained in good condition
c. Yearly Registration on the vehicle
d. Familiarization of the Quick Guide of the Petron Fleet Card

9. All vehicle shall be parked in the NMIS compound after travel, except on official travel.
