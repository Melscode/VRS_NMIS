Vehicle Reservation Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is a NMIS_VRS Account, and why do I need one?

A NMIS_VRS Account lets you sign in to the System to save and manage your personal and reservation information. You need to create an Account to reserve a vehicle.

2. What is Reservation?

A reservation lets you be among the first to reserve a place in line when a new vehicle launches from NMIS.

3. Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes. You can cancel your reservation at any time.
